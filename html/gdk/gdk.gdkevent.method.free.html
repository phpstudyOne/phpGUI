GdkEvent::free

void free();

Frees the event, freeing or decrementing any resources associated with it. Note that this method should only be called on GdkEvent objects returned from methods or functions such as Gdk::event_peek(), Gdk::event_get() and Gdk::event_get_graphics_expose().
