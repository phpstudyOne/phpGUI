GdkWindowEdge

Determines a window edge or corner.

Value

Symbolic name

Description

  0Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_NORTH_WEST the top left corner.
  1Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_NORTH the top edge.
  2Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_NORTH_EAST the top right corner.
  3Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_WEST the left edge.
  4Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_EAST the right edge.
  5Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_SOUTH_WEST the lower left corner.
  6Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_SOUTH the lower edge.
  7Gdk::WINDOW_EDGE_SOUTH_EAST the lower right corner.
