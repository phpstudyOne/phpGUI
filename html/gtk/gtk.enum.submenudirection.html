GtkSubmenuDirection

GtkSubmenuDirection is deprecated and should not be used in newly-written code.

Indicates the direction a sub-menu will appear.

Value

Symbolic name

Description

  1Gtk::DIRECTION_LEFT A sub-menu will appear at the left side of the parent menu.
  2Gtk::DIRECTION_RIGHT A sub-menu will appear at the right side of the parent menu.
